# Block Page Settings

This page can be fully customized in **Settings → Block Page Settings** to match your store’s branding and communication style.

### 1\. Block Page Title

[Section titled “1. Block Page Title”](https://docs.mida-app.io/fraud-filter/rules-management/block-page-settings/#1-block-page-title)

- The main heading that appears at the top of the block page.
- Example: _Access Denied_, _This store is not available in your region_.

![](https://docs.mida-app.io/_astro/Screenshot%202025-09-23%20at%2015.03.16.DkYfKn8y_ZMCE65.webp)

* * *

### 2\. Block Page Message

[Section titled “2. Block Page Message”](https://docs.mida-app.io/fraud-filter/rules-management/block-page-settings/#2-block-page-message)

- The body text shown under the title.

- Supports **basic HTML and Markdown**.

- Use it to explain why access is denied and how to contact you.

- Example:


> “The site owner has restricted access from your location. If you believe this is a mistake, please contact us.”


![](https://docs.mida-app.io/_astro/Screenshot%202025-09-23%20at%2015.04.00.B4ksbclz_1vcl7M.webp)

* * *

### 3\. Logo / Image

[Section titled “3. Logo / Image”](https://docs.mida-app.io/fraud-filter/rules-management/block-page-settings/#3-logo--image)

- Upload your store logo or an image to display on the block page.
- Supported formats: **.png, .jpg**.
- Recommended size: under 1MB for fast loading.

![](https://docs.mida-app.io/_astro/Screenshot%202025-09-23%20at%2015.04.28.BLt_jqt4_kio36.webp)

* * *

### 4\. Background Color

[Section titled “4. Background Color”](https://docs.mida-app.io/fraud-filter/rules-management/block-page-settings/#4-background-color)

- Choose a background color that matches your brand.
- Tip: Keep text contrast high for readability.

![](https://docs.mida-app.io/_astro/Screenshot%202025-09-23%20at%2015.05.15.BQ-n0qIH_Z130oKc.webp)

* * *

### 5\. Overlay Mode

[Section titled “5. Overlay Mode”](https://docs.mida-app.io/fraud-filter/rules-management/block-page-settings/#5-overlay-mode)

![](https://docs.mida-app.io/_astro/Screenshot%202025-09-23%20at%2015.06.25.BbsTEp_F_1LXjQL.webp)

- **If enabled**: Visitors will see a **blocking overlay** on top of the entire site.
- They cannot click or interact with any page elements.
- Useful when you want to “soft block” visitors while still showing the storefront behind a shield.

* * *

### 6\. MIDA Signature

[Section titled “6. MIDA Signature”](https://docs.mida-app.io/fraud-filter/rules-management/block-page-settings/#6-mida-signature)

![](https://docs.mida-app.io/_astro/image%20(2).CQH0sVOD_Z1yyX8L.webp)

- By default, the block page includes a small **“Protected by MIDA Fraud IP Blocker”** footer.
- You can toggle this **on/off** in settings via Contact us.
