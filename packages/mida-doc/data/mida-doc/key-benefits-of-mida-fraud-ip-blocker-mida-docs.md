# Key Benefits of MIDA Fraud IP Blocker

### Enhanced Security

[Section titled “Enhanced Security”](https://docs.mida-app.io/fraud-filter/overview/benefits/#enhanced-security)

- **Block unwanted traffic** based on IP, country, region, ISP, or device.
- **Prevent unauthorized access** to specific pages (e.g., products, collections, admin routes).
- **Protect storefront content** with anti-copy measures and custom block pages.
- **Ensure business continuity** by keeping attackers and bots away while allowing legitimate customers to shop freely.

* * *

### Fraud Prevention

[Section titled “Fraud Prevention”](https://docs.mida-app.io/fraud-filter/overview/benefits/#fraud-prevention)

- **Detect suspicious behavior** using real-time visitor analytics (IP, VPN/Proxy flags, device details).
- **Identify risky orders** by leveraging Shopify’s Fraud Analysis data combined with your own rules.
- **Build custom fraud rules** (planned) to automatically blacklist malicious IPs or cancel high-risk orders.
- **Minimize chargebacks and losses** by proactively filtering traffic before checkout.

* * *

### Access Control

[Section titled “Access Control”](https://docs.mida-app.io/fraud-filter/overview/benefits/#access-control)

- **Whitelist trusted visitors** (VIP customers, partners, or internal teams) by IP, country, ISP, product, or collection.
- **Blacklist harmful sources** to ensure they cannot place orders or view sensitive content.
- **Control rule priority** so that critical access policies are always respected.

* * *

### The Result

[Section titled “The Result”](https://docs.mida-app.io/fraud-filter/overview/benefits/#the-result)

With MIDA Fraud IP Blocker, you gain:

- A **safer storefront** protected from malicious visitors.
- **Fewer fraudulent orders** and reduced operational losses.
