# How much does it cost to use the app?

Welcome to MIDA! We offer a range of pricing plans tailored to suit your needs, whether you run a small business or lead a larger enterprise. Here’s a detailed breakdown of our pricing plans:

- **Free Plan:**

Our **Free** Plan is perfect for new stores or businesses with low traffic who are just getting started with analytics. If you want to see how visitors interact with your store through replays and heatmaps — without diving into complex data — this is the right starting point.

This plan offers up to **300** monthly sessions, along with live recordings, all heatmaps, cart & checkout tracking, funnel analytics, AI session summaries, 30-day replay data storage, and lifetime analytics data storage.

- **Basic Plan ($9.9/mo):**

Our **Basic** Plan is designed for merchants who are more familiar with the tool and have stable, growing traffic. If you want to go beyond just watching replays and start understanding _why_ visitors behave the way they do — filtering by segments, spotting patterns, and reading high-level insights — this plan is built for you.

It includes all features in the Free Plan, plus up to **5,000** monthly sessions, bounce tracking, advanced filters, priority support, AI email report, AI analytics summaries, 30 AI session summaries, and 60-day heatmap data storage with lifetime analytics storage.

An **AI Assistant** add-on is available for an additional **+$5/mo**.

- **Growth Plan ($24.9/mo):**

Our **Growth** Plan is for merchants who already understand their data and are ready to actively optimize for conversion. If you have a growth mindset and want to test UX changes, measure their impact, and focus specifically on what drives purchases, this is the plan for you.

It includes all features in the Basic Plan, plus up to **15,000** monthly sessions, revenue click tracking, conversion heatmaps, priority support, 90 AI session summaries, 60-day heatmap data storage, and lifetime analytics storage.

An **AI Assistant** add-on is available for an additional **+$10/mo**.

- **Enterprise Plan ($44.9/mo):**

Our **Enterprise** Plan is built for large brands and Shopify Plus stores with high traffic and dedicated analytics needs. If your team needs deep checkout tracking, bulk data operations, and AI-powered insights to support strategic decisions at scale, this plan has everything you need.

It includes all features in the Growth Plan, plus up to **40,000** monthly sessions, checkout recording (Shopify Plus only), API data export, bulk heatmap export, priority support, unlimited AI session summaries, 60-day heatmap data storage, and lifetime analytics storage.

An **AI Assistant** add-on is available for an additional **+$15/mo**.

All of our paid plans come with a **7-day free trial**, so you can try out MIDA and see if it’s the right fit for your business. To subscribe to a paid plan, simply login to MIDA, navigate to the Pricing Page from the Navigation Bar, choose the plan that best suits your needs, and click “ **Subscribe.**”

We also offer a **free plan** for **Development and Trial stores**, which allows you to use our app completely free of charge. If you’re not sure which plan is right for you, our team is always available to answer any questions you may have and help you choose the plan that best suits your needs.
