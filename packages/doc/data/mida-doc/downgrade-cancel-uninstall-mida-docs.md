# Downgrade, Cancel & Uninstall

If you need to reduce your plan or leave MIDA, here’s exactly what happens to your access and your data. The short version: **you keep what you’ve paid for until the end of your current billing cycle**, and your data isn’t deleted the moment you leave.

## A quick note on how billing works

[Section titled “A quick note on how billing works”](https://docs.mida-app.io/recordings/subscription-and-billing/downgrade-cancel-uninstall/#a-quick-note-on-how-billing-works)

MIDA billing runs on your Shopify billing cycle. Two principles apply across everything below:

- **Reductions don’t take effect immediately.** When you downgrade, your current plan stays active until the end of the billing cycle you’ve already paid for. The lower plan applies only after that.
- **Your data is retained for a window, not deleted instantly.** Even after you downgrade or uninstall, there’s a period where your data is kept — and reinstalling within that window restores it.

## Downgrading your plan

[Section titled “Downgrading your plan”](https://docs.mida-app.io/recordings/subscription-and-billing/downgrade-cancel-uninstall/#downgrading-your-plan)

You can move to a lower plan at any time. When you do, you’ll see a confirmation screen explaining what changes and when.

### Downgrading to a lower paid plan

[Section titled “Downgrading to a lower paid plan”](https://docs.mida-app.io/recordings/subscription-and-billing/downgrade-cancel-uninstall/#downgrading-to-a-lower-paid-plan)

When you downgrade from a higher paid plan to a lower one:

- Your **current plan stays active until the end of your billing cycle**.
- The confirmation screen shows your current billing end date so you know exactly when the change takes effect.
- After your billing cycle ends, the lower plan’s session and data limits apply.

### Downgrading to the Free plan

[Section titled “Downgrading to the Free plan”](https://docs.mida-app.io/recordings/subscription-and-billing/downgrade-cancel-uninstall/#downgrading-to-the-free-plan)

When you downgrade from a paid plan to Free:

- Your **current paid plan stays active until the end of your billing cycle** — you don’t lose paid features early.
- After your billing cycle ends, your account moves to the Free plan, and your session and data availability follow the Free plan limits.

## Cancelling your subscription

[Section titled “Cancelling your subscription”](https://docs.mida-app.io/recordings/subscription-and-billing/downgrade-cancel-uninstall/#cancelling-your-subscription)

On MIDA, cancelling your subscription means **downgrading to the Free plan** — your account stays active on Free rather than being closed. This means you keep core features like live recordings, all heatmap pages, and basic funnel analytics, just at Free plan limits.

To fully stop tracking and remove MIDA from your store, you’ll want to **uninstall** the app (see below).

If you’d like to pause paid billing but keep MIDA running, downgrading to Free is the simplest path — follow the “Downgrading to the Free plan” steps above.

## Uninstalling the app

[Section titled “Uninstalling the app”](https://docs.mida-app.io/recordings/subscription-and-billing/downgrade-cancel-uninstall/#uninstalling-the-app)

Uninstalling removes MIDA from your store. When you uninstall, **tracking and session recording stop immediately** — but what happens to your existing data depends on whether you’re on a paid or Free plan.

### If you’re on a paid plan

[Section titled “If you’re on a paid plan”](https://docs.mida-app.io/recordings/subscription-and-billing/downgrade-cancel-uninstall/#if-youre-on-a-paid-plan)

- Tracking and recording stop right away.
- Your **paid access and analytics data are kept until the end of your billing cycle**.
- If you **reinstall before your billing cycle ends**, both your premium access and your existing analytics data are fully restored.
- After your billing cycle ends, your data is removed.

### If you’re on the Free plan

[Section titled “If you’re on the Free plan”](https://docs.mida-app.io/recordings/subscription-and-billing/downgrade-cancel-uninstall/#if-youre-on-the-free-plan)

- Tracking and recording stop right away.
- Your **analytics data is kept for 7 days**.
- If you **reinstall within 7 days**, your existing data is restored.
- After 7 days, your analytics data is permanently deleted.

## At a glance

[Section titled “At a glance”](https://docs.mida-app.io/recordings/subscription-and-billing/downgrade-cancel-uninstall/#at-a-glance)

| Action | When it takes effect | What happens to your data |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Downgrade to lower paid plan | End of billing cycle | New plan limits apply after cycle ends |
| Downgrade to Free | End of billing cycle | Free plan limits apply after cycle ends |
| Uninstall (paid) | Tracking stops immediately | Data kept until end of billing cycle; reinstall restores everything |
| Uninstall (Free) | Tracking stops immediately | Data kept 7 days; reinstall within 7 days restores it |
