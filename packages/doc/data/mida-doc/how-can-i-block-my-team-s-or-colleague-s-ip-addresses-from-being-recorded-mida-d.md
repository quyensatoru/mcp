# How can I block my team's or colleague's IP addresses from being recorded?

If you want to keep your analytics clean and free from internal traffic, MIDA gives you a couple of ways to block your team’s or colleagues’ IP addresses from being recorded.

**On a Free Plan:**

You can manually block individual recordings directly from the session list. Simply identify the session you wish to exclude and click **“Block Recording of Visitor”** on the right side of the recording. This is a quick way to prevent specific internal sessions from being tracked.

**On a Paid Plan (Basic and above):**

The full **IP Exclusion** feature is available on all paid plans. With this, you can manage exclusions for multiple IP addresses as well as entire countries — giving you more control over your data accuracy at scale.
