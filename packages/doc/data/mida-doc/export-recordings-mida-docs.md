# Export Recordings

### **To export all recordings, follow these steps:**

[Section titled “To export all recordings, follow these steps:”](https://docs.mida-app.io/recordings/session-replays/export-recordings/#to-export-all-recordings-follow-these-steps)

**Step 1**: Go to the “ **Session Replays**” section of the app.

![](https://docs.mida-app.io/_astro/image%20(51).BwveEsbe_Z2cbrYg.webp)

**Step 2**: Click on the button “ **Download”**.

![](https://docs.mida-app.io/_astro/image%20(62).DALwoGKE_VSY8.webp)

Then a csv file will be downloaded to your device. You can see the information of all sessions here.

![](https://docs.mida-app.io/_astro/image%20(134).sYIEjkPN_ZC3EHW.webp)

### **To export selected recordings, follow these steps:**

[Section titled “To export selected recordings, follow these steps:”](https://docs.mida-app.io/recordings/session-replays/export-recordings/#to-export-selected-recordings-follow-these-steps)

**Step 1**: Go to the “ **Recording Replays**” section of the app.

![](https://docs.mida-app.io/_astro/image%20(51).BwveEsbe_Z2cbrYg.webp)

**Step 2**: Select recordings that you want to download.

![](https://docs.mida-app.io/_astro/image%20(57).CJEsBxOD_D5gqX.webp)

Then choose **Export as .csv** in the dropdown list of Actions. A csv file with information of selected recordings will be downloaded to your device.

![](https://docs.mida-app.io/_astro/image%20(65).M9_y5uKx_Z2jGcNr.webp)

### **To export recordings of a specific visitor, follow these steps:**

[Section titled “To export recordings of a specific visitor, follow these steps:”](https://docs.mida-app.io/recordings/session-replays/export-recordings/#to-export-recordings-of-a-specific-visitor-follow-these-steps)

**Step 1**: Go to the “ **Visitors**” section of the app.

**Step 2**: Click on the visitor that you want to view details.

![](https://docs.mida-app.io/_astro/visitor.DK6YT4g8_ZzCN8W.webp)

**Step 3a**: Click on the button **“Download”** to download all sessions.

![](https://docs.mida-app.io/_astro/image%20(66).Bc2kqQ2i_Z2gFWYR.webp)

**Step 3b**: Select **specific recordings** and click on **Export as .csv** in the dropdown Actions.

![](https://docs.mida-app.io/_astro/image%20(67).Bp4yqCnc_X1Goa.webp)
