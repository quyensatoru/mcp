# Delete Recordings

### **To delete a single recording, follow these steps:**

[Section titled “To delete a single recording, follow these steps:”](https://docs.mida-app.io/recordings/session-replays/delete-recordings/#to-delete-a-single-recording-follow-these-steps)

**Step 1**: Go to the “ **Recording Replays**” section of the app.

![](https://docs.mida-app.io/_astro/image%20(51).BwveEsbe_Z2cbrYg.webp)

**Step 2**: Choose the recording you want to delete from the list of recordings available.

![](https://docs.mida-app.io/_astro/image%20(52).CAplAf33_RJOPC.webp)

**Step 3**: Click the “Delete” button.

![](https://docs.mida-app.io/_astro/image%20(53)._JrqEXnC_ZC2XI4.webp)

**Step 4**: Confirm that you want to delete the recording.

![](https://docs.mida-app.io/_astro/image%20(54).B-CkY1Uq_MjnKv.webp)

Then a toast will inform you of successful delete.

![](https://docs.mida-app.io/_astro/image%20(55).BvqmeZlk_2dDx4y.webp)

### **To mass delete recordings, follow these steps:**

[Section titled “To mass delete recordings, follow these steps:”](https://docs.mida-app.io/recordings/session-replays/delete-recordings/#to-mass-delete-recordings-follow-these-steps)

**Step 1**: Go to the “ **Recording Replays**” section of the app.

![](https://docs.mida-app.io/_astro/image%20(51).BwveEsbe_Z2cbrYg.webp)

**Step 2**: Select the recordings you want to delete by ticking the checkboxes next to them.

![](https://docs.mida-app.io/_astro/image%20(57).CJEsBxOD_D5gqX.webp)

**Step 3**: Choose the “Delete” action from the options available.

![](https://docs.mida-app.io/_astro/image%20(58).9yYlOIDa_Zgomo0.webp)

**Step 4**: Confirm that you want to delete the selected recordings.

![](https://docs.mida-app.io/_astro/image%20(59).D6uWS_UG_Z1D9FwX.webp)

Then a toast will inform you of successful delete.

![](https://docs.mida-app.io/_astro/image%20(55).BvqmeZlk_2dDx4y.webp)

You have successfully deleted the recording(s). Please note that once a recording is deleted, it cannot be recovered.
