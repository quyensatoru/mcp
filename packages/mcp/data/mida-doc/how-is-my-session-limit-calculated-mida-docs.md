# How is my session limit calculated?

A **session recording** captures a “video” of a user’s real-time behavior during their online session. These recordings can be stored, analyzed, and filtered for analytical purposes.

Each session is assigned a unique **visitor ID** and can include one or multiple **pageviews**. For example, if a visitor adds a product to their cart starting from the homepage, that session will include three pageviews (the homepage, product page, and cart page).

The number of sessions that can be stored depends on your current pricing plan, with the session limit resetting on the first day of each month. For instance, if you are on the Basic plan and subscribed on July 15th, you will have 1,000 sessions recorded for the remainder of that month.

Additionally, old recordings are automatically after 1 month or 2 months based on your current pricing plan. For example, if you are on the Basic plan, in June 30nd, only sessions created from May 1st onward will be retained.
