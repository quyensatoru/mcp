# Will the app slow down my store?

No, the app is designed to be lightweight and optimized for performance.

MIDA Fraud Filter, IP Blocker runs asynchronously and only activates on specific requests, so it has minimal to no impact on your store’s speed or loading time. Your store will remain fast and responsive for all visitors.
