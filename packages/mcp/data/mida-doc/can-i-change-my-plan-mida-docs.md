# Can I change my plan?

Yes — you can change your plan at any time. Go to the **MIDA Recording Replay, Heatmap** app > **Pricing plan** \> select your desired plan.

Upgrades take effect immediately, so your new limits and features unlock right away. Downgrades apply at the end of your current billing cycle — your current plan stays active until then, and the new plan’s limits apply afterward. See [How to Upgrade](https://docs.mida-app.io/recordings/subscription-and-billing/how-to-upgrade/) and [Downgrade, Cancel & Uninstall](https://docs.mida-app.io/recordings/subscription-and-billing/downgrade-cancel-uninstall/) for details.

Shopify manages all billing for app charges. For billing questions you can visit [**Shopify App charges**](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/your-account/manage-billing/your-invoice/apps#app-prorating-upgrades-and-downgrades) or contact **Shopify Support** first.
