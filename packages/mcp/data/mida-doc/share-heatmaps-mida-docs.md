# Share Heatmaps

**To share heatmap(s) from MIDA, follow these steps:**

**Step 1:** Go to the “ **Heatmaps**” section of the app.

![](https://docs.mida-app.io/_astro/image%20(317).DimiV7Ec_1TPyBa.webp)

**Step 2:** Filter the date range and select Device type of Heatmaps.

![](https://docs.mida-app.io/_astro/image%20(318).lRRTlMge_2mzNRd.webp)

**Step 3:** Choose the page that you want to analyze from the list of pages available.

![](https://docs.mida-app.io/_astro/image%20(327).B7FW6caf_23PHGG.webp)

**Step 4:** Choose the type of heatmap you want to use from the available options: **Click, Move, Scroll, or Area.**

![](https://docs.mida-app.io/_astro/image.BjyXxLHd_Z212eth.webp)

**Step 5:** Click on **Share** button and **Copy** link to share.

A pop-up will display the **Share Link** and you can **Copy** it. Once you’ve done copied, click **Done** to close the pop up.

![](https://docs.mida-app.io/_astro/share%20heatmaps%20(1).JRteIXvG_256ab0.webp)
