# Can I subscribe to an annual plan?

Currently, we do not offer an annual plan. However, we are working on introducing this option in the near future.

We appreciate your interest in our app and we will keep you updated as soon as we have an annual plan available. In the meantime, we offer monthly pricing plans that provide flexibility for your business needs.
