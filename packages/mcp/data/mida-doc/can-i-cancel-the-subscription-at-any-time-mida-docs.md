# Can I cancel the subscription at any time?

Yes — you can cancel or uninstall at any time. On MIDA, “cancelling” means downgrading to the **Free plan**, so your account stays active with core features rather than being closed. If you’re on a paid plan, you keep your paid features until the end of your current billing cycle — you won’t lose access early. For the full details on what happens to your access and data, see [Downgrade, Cancel & Uninstall](https://docs.mida-app.io/recordings/subscription-and-billing/downgrade-cancel-uninstall/).

Before you go, we’d genuinely like the chance to help with any issue first — many concerns are quick to resolve.
